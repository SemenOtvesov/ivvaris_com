About

Vyacheslav was born in 1985. He graduated from the art school named after Stupin in the city of Arzamas. Works on paintings in the Italian style. The results are striking in their tenderness and sensuality. The multi-layer glazing technique gives a greater richness of color combinations, which gives a unique elegance to the works.
